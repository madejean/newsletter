Microblogging: three core values for organizations:
  • Sharing information: "There are doughnuts in the kitchen!" or "Our town hall meeting starts in 30 minutes. Please post your questions."
  • Sharing small amounts of information: "Check out this really interesting article on applying content strategy to enterprise social tools."
  • Facilitating conversations among multiple people: Marc posts a question about an upcoming product launch. John replies with as much information as he has, and @Mentions Christine to invite her to the conversation to contribute what she knows as well. Afterward, when Taylor searches the intranet for info on the product launch, that microblog conversation comes up and answers his questions, too.
